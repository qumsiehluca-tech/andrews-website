About / Founder / Approach Reading 005 / 005
[About]

Built for the way investors work.

Meridian Specialty Insurance was founded on a simple observation — traditional brokers aren't built for real estate investors. They want stabilized properties, owner-occupants, and 12-month leases. They don't know what to do with a vacant duplex, a half-finished rehab, or a 90-day fix-and-flip.

We do.

Our specialty is builders risk, vacant property, and short-term real estate coverage — the policies most carriers consider too messy, too short, or too unusual to bother with. We've built relationships with carriers who actually want this business, so you get competitive pricing on coverage that fits the way you operate: fast, flexible, and unapologetically investor-focused.

Fix-and-flippers, ground-up builders, BRRRR investors, and anyone whose deals don't fit a standard policy form — we built this agency for you.

Real estate moves fast. Closings don't wait on underwriters. We've structured our intake, carrier relationships, and submission process to turn quotes around in 24 hours or less.

Services / 01 / Builders Risk Available now
[Service / 001]

Builders Risk.

Coverage for property under construction or renovation — designed for fix-and-flippers, ground-up builders, and rehab investors. Whether you're demoing studs or pouring foundation, our builders risk policies protect the structure, materials, and work in progress against fire, theft, vandalism, and more.

We write 3, 6, and 12 month terms for residential and commercial projects, with flexible mid-policy options if your timeline shifts.

// Coverage

Structure, Materials & Labor

The building under construction, materials on-site, and in some cases materials in transit or temporary storage. Protection from foundation through certificate of occupancy.

// Perils

Basic or Special Form

Choose between named-peril (basic) or open-peril (special form) coverage based on your risk tolerance and budget. Most flips work well on basic; ground-up builds often prefer special.

// Term

3, 6, or 12 Months

Short-term coverage that matches your project timeline. Extensions available if work runs over — common, and we plan for it.

// Property

Residential or Commercial

Single-family rehabs, multifamily new construction, mixed-use, and small commercial projects all welcome.

Services / 02 / Vacant Property Available now
[Service / 002]

Vacant Property.

Most standard homeowners and landlord policies exclude vacancy after 30 to 60 days. If your property is sitting empty between tenants, in probate, awaiting renovation, or just hasn't sold yet, you're likely uninsured exactly when you need coverage most.

Our vacant property policies fill that gap — with flexible terms, no occupancy penalties, and coverage tailored to the realities of real estate investing.

// Use Case

Between Tenants or Buyers

Properties listed for sale, awaiting a new tenant, in turnover, or held vacant intentionally during a hold strategy.

// Use Case

Pre-Renovation Holds

Just closed but not yet started renovations? Get vacant coverage now and switch to builders risk when work begins.

// Use Case

Probate & Estate

Inherited or estate-held properties often sit vacant for months. We cover the gap until the property is sold or transferred.

// Coverage

Fire, Theft, Vandalism

Named-peril and special-form options available. Liability coverage can be added for an additional premium.

Insure the vacant property.

Don't wait for a standard policy to lapse vacancy coverage. Get the right policy from day one.
